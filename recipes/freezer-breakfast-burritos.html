Freezer Breakfast Burritos

Description

Flavorful turkey meatballs coated in the most delicious sweet and spicy sauce made with a combination of sriracha, soy sauce, ginger, honey and garlic. The honey and soy sauce in this recipe really balance out the heat perfectly, but if this sauce is too hot for you, I recommend just cutting the sriracha in half.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb breakfast or pork sausage
  • 2 cups frozen diced potatoes
  • 6 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp half and half
  • 8 medium tortillas
  • 3/4 cup shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. In a medium pan, cook sausage until done. Place on a plate and set aside.
  2. Thaw potatoes and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add a tablespoon of butter and melt.
  4. In a mixing bowl, whisk eggs with half and half. Season with salt and pepper. Pour egg mixture into the pan with butter. Gently swirling with a spatula or wooden spoon, cook eggs until they reach desired texture. Remove from pan onto plate. Set aside.
  5. Lay out tortillas on a cutting board. Top with equal amounts of filling, starting with eggs, then sausage, then potatoes. Top with cheese. Fold in sides of the tortilla to the center. Roll up burrito-style. Wrap in a sheet of freezer paper and place in a ziploc bag.
  6. Freeze for up to 3 months.
  7. To reheat: unwrap burrito and set on a microwave safe plate. Heat for 45 to 60 seconds or until heated through.
